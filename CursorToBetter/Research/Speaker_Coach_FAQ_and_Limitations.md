# Speaker Coach FAQ, Limitations & Privacy

**Source:** [Microsoft Support - Rehearse your slide show with Speaker Coach](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/rehearse-your-slide-show-with-speaker-coach-cd7fc941-5c3b-498c-a225-83ef3f64f07b)

## Supported Languages
*   **General**: Currently, Speaker Coach understands **English only** and requires the Office user interface language to be set to English [1].
*   **Pronunciation Feature**: Specifically available only for the **US English** market [1].
*   **Future Plans**: Microsoft is working to expand to Spanish, French, and Japanese [1].

## Platform Differences
*   **Web**: Generally receives the most up-to-date features first.
*   **Desktop Apps**: Provides feedback on pace, monotone, filler words, speech refinement, originality, and inclusiveness.
*   **Mobile (iOS/Android)**:
    *   Available on iPhone, iPad, and Android tablets.
    *   Includes feedback on pace, pitch, filler words, speech refinements, originality, repetitive language, and inclusiveness.
    *   *Note*: Repetitive language and Pronunciation features are "coming soon" for mobile [1].

## Privacy & Technology
*   **Technology**: Powered by **Microsoft Speech Services** (cloud-enhanced feature) [1].
*   **Data Usage**: Speech utterances are sent to Microsoft to provide the service [1].
*   **Government Cloud**: Speaker Coach is **not available** for Government Community Cloud (GCC) customers [1].

## Frequently Asked Questions
*   **Is it free?** Yes, Speaker Coach is currently in free preview for everyone, even without an Office subscription (on Web, Android, iOS) [1].
*   **Does it add timings?** No. Unlike the "Rehearse Timings" feature, Speaker Coach is strictly for practice and feedback; it does not automate slide transitions [1].
